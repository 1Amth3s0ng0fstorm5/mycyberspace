ICT


And what its all about


ICT is a profession divided into 3 major groups and the subgroups under those 3. because of the volume of subgroups only the major 3 will be covered and a few subgroups as examples. Now to begin with the 3 major fields are; Software/Web Development, IT Consultancy and Data Science. starting with Software/Web Development, which, as its name suggest is all about the development of applications And websites, which is what we are doing right now. IT Consultancy works on bridging ICT with major companies, their job involves helping these companies build the backend systems that run their buisness to even explaining how that systems works. Finally we have data science and to cover everything would take up 2 wikipedia pages so in short its all about taking data and converting it into real world information which requires a lot of algorithmic thinking and requires you to answer critical questions.

and before anyone asks, no i did not forget AI programming, however i do not consider this a major branch as it falls into Software/Web Development since the code is just filled with over-glorified if statements.
