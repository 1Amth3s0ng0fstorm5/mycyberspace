  • How can I order something off of the HZ Webshop?

    first goto the HZ Webshop.
    Then log in.
    Then search for your desired product.
    Click "add to shopping cart".
    Click "checkout".
    Then choose your method of payment.
    click next and then follow the payment instructions.

    For more questions go here.
  • How can I scan a document and send it to my laptop?

    Go to a HZ printer/scanner.
    Log in with your student ID card.
    Press scan after placing the desired document in the scanner.
    Press start.
    See question1 on how to log into the printer website.
    Download the files.

    For more questions go here.
  • How can I book a project space?

    First go to the Selfservice Portal here.
    Then Go To Reservations and reserve a Meeting or Project Room.
    Then select the room you want and the timeslot, then click next.
    Fill in the required information then confirm your reservation.

    For more questions go here.
  • Where can I park my car?

    You can't park your car directly by the school. There is parking available, but only for crippled/disabled people. Instead, you can park your car at the Kousteensedijk parking place, where there are parking places for employees and students, about 8 minutes walking from here. When you show your card, you can drive in.
